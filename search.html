The Murder on the Links is a work of detective fiction by Agatha Christie, first published in the US by Dodd, Mead & Co[1][2] in March 1923 and, in the
same year, in the UK by The Bodley Head in May.[3] It features Hercule Poirot and Arthur Hastings. The UK edition retailed at seven shillings and sixpence
(7/6),[3] and the US edition at $1.75.[2]
